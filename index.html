Cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrency is digital money. This type of currency uses blockchain technology, which is considered secure because it is capable of establishing distributed consensus even among untrustworthy parties.

Stocks

Investing in stocks means buying shares of ownership in a public company. Those small shares are known as the company’s stock, and by investing in that stock, you’re hoping the company grows and performs well over time.

NFT

NFTs are digital assets that act as secure documentation of ownership and can be a worthwhile investment for collectors. They can also be risky. Learn how to create, buy, and sell NFTs.
